Indian Spices & Pulses

Authentic Indian Flavors

At MMI Logitek Fzco, we take pride in sourcing and exporting the finest quality Indian spices and pulses to customers around the world. Our products are carefully selected from trusted suppliers across India, ensuring authenticity, freshness, and superior taste in every shipment.

Our Product Range

Premium Spices

  • Turmeric (Haldi) - Known for its vibrant color and health benefits
  • Red Chili Powder - Adds heat and flavor to any dish
  • Cumin Seeds (Jeera) - Essential for Indian cooking
  • Coriander Powder (Dhania) - Aromatic and flavorful
  • Garam Masala - Traditional spice blend
  • Black Pepper - The king of spices
  • Cardamom (Elaichi) - Premium quality green and black varieties
  • Cloves (Laung) - Aromatic and medicinal
  • Cinnamon (Dalchini) - Sweet and warming spice

Quality Pulses (Dal)

  • Toor Dal (Pigeon Peas) - Staple protein source
  • Moong Dal (Green Gram) - Nutritious and easy to digest
  • Chana Dal (Split Chickpeas) - Rich in protein and fiber
  • Masoor Dal (Red Lentils) - Quick cooking and nutritious
  • Urad Dal (Black Gram) - Essential for South Indian cuisine
  • Rajma (Kidney Beans) - Popular in North Indian dishes
  • Kabuli Chana (Chickpeas) - Versatile and protein-rich
