Container Trading & Leasing

At MMI Logitek Fzco, we offer cost-effective container trading and leasing solutions tailored for global logistics, storage, and custom-built applications. Whether you need containers for short-term use, long-term projects, or permanent ownership, we provide a wide range of container sizes and types to suit your specific needs.

Container Leasing Services

We specialize in leasing high-quality shipping containers for:

  • International cargo movement
  • Temporary storage solutions
  • Industrial and construction site needs
  • Government and private sector logistics

Our container leasing services are ideal for:

  • Importers & Exporters
  • Freight forwarders & NVOCCs
  • Logistics companies
  • Project-based and seasonal users

We offer flexible leasing terms, competitive pricing, and timely availability across key locations.

Container Trading

Looking to buy your own container? We deal in used and new containers, offering units that meet international ISO standards with options like:

  • Dry containers (20ft, 40ft, High Cube)
  • Open-top and flat-rack containers
  • Reefer (refrigerated) containers
  • Specialized containers for specific cargo needs

Our container units are thoroughly inspected for quality, durability, and compliance.

Porta Cabin Conversions

We also offer container modifications and Porta Cabin conversions customized to your design requirements. These versatile structures are ideal for:

  • Mobile offices
  • Site accommodations
  • Portable storage units
  • Emergency shelters

We deliver tailor-made cabin solutions using robust, weather-resistant containers to meet your exact specifications.
