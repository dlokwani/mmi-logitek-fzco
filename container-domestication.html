Container Domestication

MMI Logitek Fzco offers specialized Container Domestication Services to help businesses convert international shipping containers into domestic-use containers, ensuring full compliance with local regulations and customs requirements.

What is Container Domestication?

Container domestication is the process of converting an imported shipping container from international status to domestic status. This allows the container to be used freely within the country for storage, transportation, or other purposes without being subject to customs restrictions or re-export obligations.

Why Domesticate a Container?

  • Avoid customs penalties and demurrage charges
  • Use containers for long-term domestic storage or transport
  • Convert containers into permanent structures (offices, warehouses, etc.)
  • Eliminate the need to re-export empty containers
  • Gain full ownership and control over the container

Our Container Domestication Process

We handle the entire domestication process from start to finish:

  • Assessment of container eligibility and condition
  • Preparation and submission of required documentation
  • Coordination with customs authorities
  • Payment of applicable duties and taxes
  • Issuance of domestication certificates
  • Final clearance and release of containers

Documentation Required

  • Original Bill of Lading (B/L)
  • Commercial Invoice
  • Packing List
  • Import License (if applicable)
  • Container ownership documents
  • Customs declaration forms
