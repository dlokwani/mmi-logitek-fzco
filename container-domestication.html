Container Domestication

Container domestication is the process of converting an international shipping container into a locally registered unit for domestic use. This involves the legal nationalization of containers, allowing them to operate within the internal logistics network of a country without international documentation constraints.

At MMI Logitek Fzco, we are a trusted name in container domestication, with a proven track record and expertise built over the past 8 years.
