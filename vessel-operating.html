Vessel Operating Agent

A Vessel Operating Agent acts as the representative of a shipping line or vessel owner at a port, coordinating all operational, commercial, and administrative activities required for a vessel's call. At MMI Logitek Fzco, we provide comprehensive vessel agency services, ensuring smooth port operations and efficient turnaround times.

Our Vessel Agency Services

As your trusted vessel operating agent, we handle all aspects of vessel operations at port, including:

  • Pre-arrival documentation and port clearances
  • Coordination with port authorities and customs
  • Berth booking and pilotage arrangements
  • Cargo operations supervision
  • Crew changes and provisions supply
  • Bunker arrangements and technical support
  • Financial settlements and disbursement accounts
  • Post-departure documentation

Why Choose Our Vessel Agency Services?

  • 24/7 Operations Support - Round-the-clock availability for vessel operations
  • Experienced Team - Professional staff with deep knowledge of port operations
  • Strong Port Relationships - Established connections with port authorities and service providers

  • Transparent Reporting - Detailed documentation and financial reporting
  • Cost-Effective Solutions - Competitive rates with no hidden charges
