NVOCC Services

An NVOCC (Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier) is a freight forwarding company that operates as a carrier without owning or operating vessels. Instead, NVOCCs consolidate cargo from multiple shippers, book space on ocean carriers, and issue their own House Bill of Lading (HBL) to customers.

At MMI Logitek Fzco, we are a licensed and experienced NVOCC, providing reliable and cost-effective ocean freight solutions to businesses worldwide.

What We Offer as an NVOCC

  • Consolidated Shipping Solutions - We combine shipments from multiple clients to optimize container space and reduce costs
  • House Bill of Lading (HBL) - We issue our own HBL, giving you direct control and flexibility over your cargo
  • Competitive Freight Rates - Leveraging our strong relationships with major shipping lines, we secure the best rates for our clients
  • Global Network - With a presence across key trade routes including UAE, USA, China, Southeast Asia, and more, we ensure seamless connectivity
  • FCL & LCL Services - Whether you need Full Container Load (FCL) or Less than Container Load (LCL), we have you covered
  • Door-to-Door Delivery - End-to-end logistics support from origin to final destination

  • Dedicated customer support and real-time cargo tracking
