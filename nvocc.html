NVOCC

At MMI Logitek Fzco, we proudly operate as a Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC) — offering comprehensive ocean freight services similar to a traditional steamship line, with the flexibility and efficiency today's global trade demands.

While a steamship line owns vessels and containers, an NVOCC like us provides equivalent shipping services without owning the vessels. Instead, we specialize in space utilization, freight consolidation, and end-to-end cargo management, giving our clients the benefits of global transport without the complexity.
